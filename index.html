My history

My first years

2003-2006

Midfielder-Right/Left Winger

I played firstly in the inferiors of Barelona, and then, in 2004 I debuted in the first team and played with leyends like Ronaldinho and Deco. I made various goals and then we won the champions league 2005-2006.

2007 - 2009

False 9, Attacking Midfielder, Right Winger

This were some of the best years of my career , as, for example I made a Hat-trick in El Clasico that finished Barcelona: Lionel Messi (11', 28', 90'+1') 3-3 Ruud van Nistelrooy (5', 13' Pen)Sergio Ramos (73') Real Madrid.
I scored a Goal in the Final of the Uefa Champions League against Manchester United.
And we also won the Champions League and "El Sextete", as we won Spanish Super Cup, Uefa Super Cup, Champions League , Club World Cup, La Liga Española and La Copa del Rey.
I did also won the Ballon D'or at 2009.

Education

2010-2014

Right Winger, False 9

I won 3 Ballon D'ors (2010,2011,2012)
I won various Leagues and Cups, but the most important was the UCL won in 2011, against Man.United and my (considered) best prime in 2012, Where i socred 91 goals and 22 assists in 69 games with 113 Goal Contributions and some worse but similar stats in 2013.

2015 - 2018

Right Winger

With Suarez and Neymar Jr we won my Fourth UCL in the 2014-2015 and I won my 5th Ballon D'or but in the next UCL (2015-2016) we lost in "El vicente Calderon" against Atletico.
Then in the 2016-2017 we passed group phase easily but in Round of 16 against PSG we los 4-0 in Paris, but then, we made "La Remontada" but we lost against Juventus in Quarter Finals.
In 2017-2018, we went very well excepting for that Neymar Jr got out of the club and Barça stopped being one of the best clubs: We lost against Roma with Visitor Goal Law.

2019 - Now

Right Winger, Second

We won 2019 League and I won my 6th Ballon D'or but we faced other Remontada against us: We won in the Camp Nou 3-0 but we lost in Anfield 4-0 for the Semi-Finals of the 2018-2019 UCL because of the bad Defense.
Then, in the next(2019-2020) UCL we lost 8-2 against Bayern in Round of 16. In the 2020-2021 UCL and the last for Messi we lost against PSG at Round of 16. Then I won Copa America with Argentina and went to PSG and I won my 7th Ballon D'or. In 2022 I won the World Cup with Argentina and went to Inter Miami. I will win the 2023 Ballon D'or.
